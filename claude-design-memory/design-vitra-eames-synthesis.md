## Vitra Design Museum
- **Weil am Rhein**。Schaudepot 2016 开（Herzog & de Meuron 设计），藏 ~20,000 件（含 7,000 件家具 + Eames Office archive + Panton / Girard / Barragán archive）
- 1989 由 **Rolf Fehlbaum** 创馆，Alexander von Vegesack 1989-2010，**Mateo Kries** 接任
- *Atlas of Furniture Design*（2019, 1,000 页, 1,740 物件, 540 设计师）= 馆出版旗舰
- 当前展：Panton 百年 *Verner Panton: Form, Colour, Space* 含 Visiona II 1970 "Fantasy Landscape" 1/8 复刻

## Eames（最深的单一源）
- "Kazam!" 机器故事：1941 结婚，"在 Neutra 设计的 Westwood 公寓 spare bedroom"，Charles 把 MGM 木料偷回家，做 moulding machine，先做**自己腿的石膏模型腿夹板**
- US Navy 订 5,000 个 → 工作室从床上搬到 Santa Monica Blvd
- 关键项目：Plywood Chair (1945-46) / DCM/LCM / Wire Mesh (1951-53) / **Lounge Chair (1956, Herman Miller)** / Aluminium Group (1958) / Eames House (1949) / La Chaise (1948 with Saarinen for MoMA) / 1959 Moscow 电影 *Glimpses of the USA*
- **Eames Office archive 在 Vitra Schaudepot** —— 别的设计博物馆没有这种 archival weight

## 10 个代表设计师（按 cluster 出现频率）
1. **Eileen Gray (1878–1976)** —— 1917 *Vogue* "she stands alone, unique"；E-1027, Bibendum, Transat
2. **Verner Panton (1926–1998)** —— Cone Chair / **Panton Chair (1967/68, Vitra 第一个"无后腿单件合成材料"椅子)** / **Visiona II (1970, Bayer, "Gesamtkunstwerk")**
3. **Marcel Breuer (1902–1981)** —— 1935 到伦敦，Isokon Jack Pritchard 拒他金属做家具、逼他转胶合板 → "5 件套胶合板 ... 现在被视为 20 世纪家具设计 landmark"
4. **Arne Jacobsen (1902–1971)** —— St Catherine's College Oxford 合同**写死"landscape design and fixtures and fittings"**；Kubrick 借 Cylinda Line 当"timelessly futuristic props"
5. **Isamu Noguchi (1904–1988)** —— mulberry paper Akari lights / Martha Graham 舞美 / Zenith Radio Nurse / UNESCO stone gardens
6. **Charlotte Perriand (1903–1999)** —— 24 岁问 Le Corbusier 求职，**"we don't embroider cushions here"** 关她门；数月后回心转意；B301/B302/B306 共同设计
7. **Ettore Sottsass (1917–2007) + Memphis** —— "functionalism functionalism functionalism is not enough. Design should also be sensual and exciting." Memphis (1981-85/87, 解散时间有争议——米兰首展 1981-09，社团实际结束 1987-88) 名字来自 Bob Dylan 歌词
8. **Gio Ponti (1891–1979)** —— *Amate l'Architettura* / Superleggera (1957) / Pirelli Tower
9. **Charles Rennie Mackintosh (1868–1928)** —— Glasgow School of Art / Willow Tea Rooms / Hill House = "complete environment"
10. **Christopher Dresser (1834–1904)** —— **最早的人物**，*Studio* 杂志 1899: "perhaps the greatest of commercial designers"

## "Form follows function" → "Form follows emotion"
- **Rams** 的战后德国语："Im Grunde genommen war es zuerst unsere Aufgabe, aufzuräumen" —— 10 Thesen: **Weglassen, Ehrlichkeit, Materialgerechtigkeit**
- **Sottsass** 一代后的反推："It's not enough. Design should also be sensual and exciting"
- **Panton Visiona II** = 空间版
- **Rams 向前看**："Wir bleiben ja ständig an dem Begriff Design hängen, dabei müssten wir eigentlich von Umweltgestaltung sprechen" = design is never neutral

## 5 条可抄原则
1. **Eames 庆祝 process 不是 product**。"Kazam!" machine / 床-工作室-loft 流水线 / 电影玩具 = 交付物的一部分
2. **Design as total environment**。Mackintosh tea rooms → Panton Visiona II → Jacobsen St Catherine's = **设计师工作延伸到门把手/灯具/景观植物**
3. **Material constraint 是 originality 的引擎**。Breuer 的 plywood 椅存在因为 Pritchard 拒金属。Newson 底漆因为"noticed what nobody else noticed"
4. **Designer as bridge and as user-researcher**。Jongerius "ping-pong between head and hands" / **Goldfinger 1965 在自己 Balfron Tower 单元住两个月**（=早期 user research）/ Rams 研究牛粪叉
5. **Collection and archive 是 ongoing work**。Vitra "stetig wachsende Sammlung" + 70 作者 *Atlas* = research infrastructure

## 设计运动连接
Dresser (1834) pre-modern → Mackintosh (1868-1928) Art Nouveau 桥 → **Bauhaus 经 Breuer (1921 学徒, 1935 伦敦)** / Lubetkin 1931 London → post-war 顶峰 = Eames + Days + Jacobsen + Noguchi + Ponti → 1960s 激进批判 Superstudio / 1960s-70s 感官转折 Panton / Rams "less, but better" / Memphis (1981, Dylan) / Dixon 80s DIY / 90s-00s craft-tech Boontje+Jongerius+Grcic+Buxton / Conran+Habitat = Bauhaus 商业翻译 / Ive+iMac = 消费技术翻译
