## 7 位设计师一句话
- **Arne Jacobsen (1902–1971)** —— "**economy plus function equals style**" 丹麦代表；Series 7 chair 5M+ 销量；"I am choking on aesthetics"
- **Jean Prouvé (1901–1984)** —— blacksmith apprentice → engineer；"**design as a moral issue**"；Maxéville 工厂 "socialist principles"；1936 catalogue 是 "standard models for hospitals, schools and offices"
- **R. Buckminster Fuller (1895–1983)** —— "**more for less**"；Dymaxion Car (1933) / Deployment Unit (WWII) / **Montreal Biosphere 76m 直径 62m 高, 5.3M 访客 6 个月**
- **Konstantin Grcic (b. 1965)** —— "**defining function in human terms**" + "**formal rigour with subtle humour**"；Chair_One (2002, Magis) "**like a football**"；Mayday/Myto/OK 3 个 Compasso d'Oro
- **Matali Crasset (b. 1965)** —— "question the way we go about our daily lives"；Phytolab cube = 镜子不要，"redesign 洗澡通过触觉+嗅觉"
- **Mathias Bengtsson (b. 1971)** —— Slice 系列 "**laser-cut + hand-assembled**"；碳纤维 = NASA/Boeing 技术 + hand-made 家具
- **M/M (Paris)** —— Amzalag + Augustyniak (1992)；Yohji Yamamoto / Balenciaga / Calvin Klein / Björk / Inez & Vinoodh；"**a social commitment… a way to earn a living and also to disseminate our ideas**"

## 6 条共同主题
1. **Material honesty**。Grcic "high quality plastics rather than fake metal" / Bengtsson 碳纤维不隐藏 / Prouvé 焊钢暴露工艺
2. **Function as moral programme**。Jacobsen "economy + function = style" / Prouvé "design as a moral issue" / Fuller "more for less"
3. **Modularity and seriality**。Fuller 测地线 dome = 等边三角格子 / Prouvé 1936 catalogue "standard models for hospitals, schools and offices" / Series 7 = 5M+ units
4. **Total design / environment as object**。Jacobsen SAS Royal = "Lost Gesamtkunstwerk" / St Catherine's College = 3m 网格统一建筑-花园-门把手
5. **Production method as aesthetic**。Bengtsson laser-cut + hand-assembled = 工艺可见 / Prouvé 焊接金属显示接缝 / Montreal Biosphere 1976 烧掉 acrylic 皮肤后 "creating a beautifully legible transparency that fully reveals the ingenuity of Fuller's design"
6. **Design as a question, not a solution**。Crasset 无镜 Phytolab = 不优化洗澡，问用户"用 touch + smell 重新定义它"

## 对现代主义的反推
- **Prouvé** 故意 "avoided the domestic market" = 大批量市场不是 social leverage 在的地方
- **Grcic** 理性主义 10 年后**主动转向 computer design software + fluid forms**，Chair_One "deliberately strange" 而非 refined
- **Crasset** 质询 modernist problem-solving 的**前提**不是形式
- **Bengtsson** 用 digital-industrial hybrid 替换人本 workshop
- **M/M (Paris)** 把 typographic rigour 带入 fashion campaign = modernist grid 当 mood 工具不当 clarity 工具

## 4 条可抄决策
1. **Prouvé 公开设计是 moral issue** —— 不卖 domestic 市场 = 把 social leverage 放在批量公共项目
2. **Grcic "computer + humour"** —— 10 年理性主义后**主动转向** fluid forms
3. **Crasset "mirrorless bathing"** —— **质疑前提**不优化形式
4. **Bengtsson "carbon fibre + hand-assembled"** —— 数字工业+手工 hybrid = 工艺可见

## Why it matters
"modernist conviction that the designer's job is to integrate form, function, and process—but each generation has been willing to redefine what those three words mean." —— **modular 长青 + 道德 function + 工艺可见 + 质疑前提 = 7 人 90 年的共同遗产**
