It’s Nice That About Since we were founded in 2007, It’s Nice That has become the leading platform to inspire creativity around the world. Across our online platform, and through our events and social channels, we reach an audience of over 2 million people every month – from juniors joining the industry to creative thought leaders. As well as covering inspirational work and projects on the It’s Nice That website, we also produce social media video content, a monthly event in London and New York called Nicer Tuesdays, and the It’s Nice That Podcast. Alongside all of this, we use our creative expertise to help brands communicate their messages and products to our audience through partnerships and other forms of advertising. Inside The Company It’s Nice That began in 2007 as a university project, responding to a brief to “put something positive into the world”. Starting out as a blog, it quickly grew to become one of the world’s leading creative publications. It is now regarded as a go-to for boundary-pushing creative work and news, and has become a well-respected industry staple with positivity at its core. Today, the team consists of about 15 full-time members – which often expands for projects – spread across editorial, creative, project management and new business teams. Culture and Benefits The company has a democratic and collaborative spirit, where everyone’s ideas are heard. If you are passionate about something, you are given the tools, trust and encouragement to make it happen. They are proud to offer several perks, which include a generous holiday allowance, learning and development opportunities, flexible working hours, a cycle-to-work scheme, as well as discounted gym memberships, among many more.
