The film is directed by Gary Hustwit. Objectified premièred at the South By Southwest Festival on March 14, 2009. According to Swiss Dots Production, the film is the second part of the three-film series Design Trilogy,[1] the first being Helvetica about the famous typeface, and the third and final film being the documentary Urbanized.
