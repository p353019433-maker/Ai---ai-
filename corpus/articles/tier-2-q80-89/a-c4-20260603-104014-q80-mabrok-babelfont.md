Because typography is the art of speaking without sound. Once, I saw the letter “a” shaped like a pear. And an “m” with legs. It was a living typeface, a real one. At Babelfont, we craft narratives before even writing words. So yes, a good typeface is like a good phrase: you don’t explain it you print it.
