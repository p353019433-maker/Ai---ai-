SN Skandia.pdf SN Skandia Narrow.pdf SN Skandia Condensed.pdf Buy License About Store Norske Skandia is a rational geometric typeface designed for the northern mindset. Rooted in the clarity and utility of Scandinavian modernism, Skandia builds on its heritage to achieve a spirit of clarity and lasting relevance for today’s digital world. Designed with a focus on versatility and sustainability, Skandia is crafted to remain relevant in an ever-evolving visual landscape. Its straightforward forms and understated elegance convey simplicity, functionality, and a quiet confidence, qualities that make Skandia an appealing choice for branding, advertising, and digital design. With carefully balanced contrast and proportions, Skandia ensures excellent readability on screens and in print, adapting seamlessly to a wide range of applications. The Skandia superfamily includes Skandia, Skandia Mono, Skandia Narrow, and Skandia Condensed, each available in eight weights with matching italics. Skandia’s clean design and subtle personality make it a reliable tool for crafting cohesive, modern, and timeless visual identities. Skandia began with the discovery of a long-lost Scandinavian neo-grotesque originally designed for the Norwegian National Railway. Read more about Skandia’s roots and design process here.
