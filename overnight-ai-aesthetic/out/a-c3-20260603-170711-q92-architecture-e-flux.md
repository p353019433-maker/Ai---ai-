Since its inception, e-flux Architecture has maintained a dynamic program of projects and events in collaboration with leading institutions and practitioners. Editorial content commissioned and published by e-flux Architecture consistently showcases rigorous, critical, sincere, and engaged work being produced today in and around the fields of architecture, urbanism, and design. FAQ What are e-flux Architecture announcements? e-flux Architecture announcements are a direct e-mailing of text and image press releases to our growing database of art, architecture, urban and design professionals.
