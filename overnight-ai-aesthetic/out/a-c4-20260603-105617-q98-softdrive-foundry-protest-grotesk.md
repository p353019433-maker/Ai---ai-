Softdrive Foundry | Protest Grotesk Protest Grotesk About the Typeface Protest Grotesk is a friendly yet straightforward low-contrast sans serif, available in a wide range of weights. Inspired by late 19th- and early 20th-century geometric sans-serifs like Kabel and Futura, while also drawing on legibility focused classics such as Akzidenz Grotesk, it adds a modern twist that works beautifully in both display and text settings. With a balance of sporty, headline ready sharpness and reliable structure, Protest Grotesk excels in both print and digital contexts, making it a versatile tool for today’s design challenges.
