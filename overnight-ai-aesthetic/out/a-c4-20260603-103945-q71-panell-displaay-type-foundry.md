Info description Panell is a punchy take on a familiar answer, just viewed from a different angle. This boxy typeface collection, with its irregular rounded corners, draws from a kind of new brutalism, inspired by concrete housing blocks, balances between a humanized and technical aesthetic. Styles range across four weights, from Regular to Bold, and seven widths, from Compressed to Expanded. The idea of stretching proportions is supported by its variable font format. Sure, the typeface might remind some of the iconic Eurostile (Microgramma), or perhaps Compacta or Earth, but Panell confidently breaks away. It has a futuristic, technical vibe: a geometric sans–serif with its own strong attitude. One standout detail is the uneven construction of the letter “o”, where one diagonal has sharper rounding, and the opposite diagonal uses a different radius. This subtle tension propels the rhythm forward and gives the typeface its original momentum. There are playful touches too, like the shortened bottom arms of “f”, “t” and “y,” which get space to shine, and these are hidden within stylistic sets. Another important source of tension comes from moments where opposites meet rounded corners appear only in specific places.
