Stephen Coles ✕ Home Page Stephen Coles is Editorial Director & Associate Curator at Letterform Archive in San Francisco. He also co-publishes Fonts In Use and Typographica. Previously, Stephen wrote the book The Anatomy of Type and was a creative director at FontShop. He shares his life with his wife, Laura Serra.
