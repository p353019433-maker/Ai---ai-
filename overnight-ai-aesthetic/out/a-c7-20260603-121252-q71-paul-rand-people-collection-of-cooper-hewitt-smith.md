While at the League he studied with George Grosz. Author of "Thoughts on Design," an influential book of design practices published in 1946, Rand incorporates collage and montage techniques to create a dynamic visual / verbal communications vocabulary. Paul Klee and Wassily Kandinsky provided a starting point for Rand who adapted formal qualities from their Modernist paintings to his graphic designs.
