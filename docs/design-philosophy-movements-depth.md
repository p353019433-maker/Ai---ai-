# 现代主义运动深度补充（循环2深度）

## 俄罗斯构成主义 Russian Constructivism
- 1915 由 Tatlin + Rodchenko 创立
- 拒绝装饰风格 → 工业材料
- 与苏维埃社会主义 + 俄国先锋派相关
- 关键词：**faktura**（材料属性）+ **tektonika**（空间存在）
- 字体设计："intended to create a reaction, and function emotionally"
- 来自俄国形式主义"making strange"（陌生化）

### El Lissitzky
- 信念：艺术应"组织"生活而非"装饰"
- 拒绝特定方向："to have neither top nor bottom"
- **Proun 作品（1919-1920s）**：UNOVIS 项目缩写
- 抽象几何绘画：Malevich 平面 + 第三维度
- "stopping point on the path of constructing a new form"
- "the 'communist foundation of steel and concrete for all the people of the earth'"
- 1923 Proun Room：让观众"走进图像"
- **"Beat the Whites with the Red Wedge" (1919)**：红三角刺穿白圆
- Picasso 评论："painting was not invented for decorating houses, but as a weapon"
- 1923 Mayakovsky《For The Voice》字体设计：现代主义排版杰作
- 早期：Yiddish 字符颜色编码
- 与 Moholy-Nagy 合作，遇见 Kurt Schwitters
- 影响：Jan Tschichold 称《The Constructor》为"finest and most important work"

### Alexander Rodchenko
- 与 Mayakovsky 合作自称 "advertising constructors"
- Mosselprom 莫斯科国营百货设计
- 放弃绘画 → 平面设计
- 用圆规直尺去除绘画笔触
- 摄影：奇角度（俯拍、仰拍）→ 震惊观众，延迟识别
- "One has to take several different shots of a subject, from different points of view"
- 1923-1928 与 Mayakovsky 设计 LEF / Novy LEF
- 1924 Lengiz 出版社海报
- 设计 Dziga Vertov《Kino Eye》(1924) 字幕

## De Stijl
- 创立：Theo van Doesburg + Piet Mondrian
- 严格限制：原色（红/蓝/黄）+ 非色（黑/白/灰）
- 形状限制：水平/垂直线 + 矩形
- Rietveld 1919 加入
- **Red and Blue Chair (1917)**：1918 受 De Stijl 影响后改色
- 1923 在 Bauhaus 展览

### Mondrian 新造型主义
- 目标：创造"universal beauty"
- "Art is higher than reality and has no direct relation to reality"
- "To approach the spiritual in art, one will make as little use as possible of reality"
- 元素：
  - 原色：红/蓝/黄
  - 原值：黑/白/灰
  - 原向：水平/垂直
- Bayley："Mondrian has come to mean Modernism. His name and his work sum up the High Modernist ideal"
- 影响：
  - 时装：YSL 1965 Mondrian 系列
  - 建筑：海牙市政厅、香港 Chun Yeung Estate
  - 软件：Piet 编程语言
  - 产品：Swatch 手表、Nike 鞋

## Wiener Werkstätte 维也纳工坊
- 1903 创立：Koloman Moser + Josef Hoffmann + Fritz Waerndorfer
- 媒体：陶瓷/时装/珠宝/家具/金属/皮革/平面艺术
- 目标："give practical and appropriate forms to all objects and then to make these unique and valuable through pleasing proportions and harmonious shapes"
- 影响：先于 Bauhaus + Art Deco
- Bauhaus 延续其几何与简约，强调可负担材料的大规模生产

## Art Deco
- 灵感：Vienna Secession + Cubism 的"bold geometric forms"
- 简化：cylinder / sphere / cone
- 风格："luxury, glamour, exuberance, faith in social and technological progress"
- 材料："rare and expensive materials such as ebony and ivory"
- 1930s → Streamline Moderne：curving forms + smooth polished surfaces
- 与现代主义对比：现代主义"strict functionality"，Art Deco 装饰+现代

## Jan Tschichold
- **《Die neue Typographie》1928**：
  - 谴责所有非 sans-serif（Grotesk）字体
  - 偏好非居中设计
  - 标准化纸张
  - 编码"new typography"规则
- "one of the most important representatives of the 'new typography'"
- 1932 转变：放弃严格信条，倾向古典主义
- 后谴责现代主义为"authoritarian and inherently fascistic"
- 后悔《新字体排印》太极端
- 战后瑞士：对 neo-grotesque 不感冒（"a revival of poorly designed models"）

## Max Bill
- 现象学而非纯理性
- 代表 20c 早期"新物理"
- 让抽象概念可被感官理解
- **"the ultimate expression of a concrete art"**
- **Ulmer Hocker (1954)**：凳子，几何精准
- "the single most decisive influence on Swiss graphic design beginning in the 1950s"
- 1953 共同创立乌尔姆设计学院（接续 Bauhaus）
- 1951 圣保罗展："lit the fuse of Brazil's artistic revolution"

## Kenya Hara
- "making known unknown" — 把熟悉变新
- Musashino Art University 教授
- **核心："emptiness" 空**（不是缺失，是潜力）
- 书《White》：白是"a color from which color has escaped, but its diversity is boundless"
- MUJI 艺术总监 2001-（接 Muji 哲学）

## 田中一光 Ikko Tanaka
- "careful balance between traditional and modern"
- 17c 京都 Rinpa 画派（装饰+鲜艳）+ 美国/欧洲
- 不愿"wholly align with an aesthetic that 'purveys an idea of beauty that's far too far away from what we are'"
- Yamashita 评："a 20th-century iteration of Rinpa"
- 风格："bold, polychromatic geometries" + "harnessing the dynamic visual potential of typography"
- 兴趣：negative space / contoured forms / bold simple geometries / vivid swaths of color
- Mazda 工作

## 对 AI 去 AI 化的启示
1. **Lissitzky "组织生活" vs "装饰生活"**：设计应有目的，不是装饰
2. **Rodchenko 圆规直尺**：AI 喜欢"手绘感"，但有时系统化更高级
3. **Mondrian 限制 = 自由**：3 色 + 水平垂直 = 反而能做出无穷变化
4. **Tschichold 字体无衬线**：但他自己后来也放弃了
5. **Hara 空 = 潜力**：留白不是"缺失"，是"可能"
6. **Tanaka 传统现代平衡**：AI 默认"全现代"或"全复古"，平衡才是美
7. **Wiener Werkstätte 比例**：美学不靠装饰，靠 proportions
8. **Art Deco 警示**：装饰太多 = 廉价感
9. **Bill 现象学**：感官先于理性，AI 输出经常"理性但无感官"
10. **Rietveld 1917 chair**：基本形 + 基本色 = 永恒设计
