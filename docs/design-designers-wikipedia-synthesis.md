## 8 个 Wikipedia 深读（一句话各一）
- **Wallpaper\* (1996-)** —— Tyler Brûlé + Alexander Geringer 创办，1997 卖 Time Warner，现 Future plc。**多收入平台**：Wallpaper* Bespoke + 100+ city guides (Phaidon) + WallpaperSTORE* (2015 e-commerce) + guest-edited October (Koons/Hadid/Lagerfeld/Lynch/Kraftwerk/Gehry/Kusama/Armani)。**杂志 = 失去领头羊的 editorial authority，向外许可其 taste**
- **Landor Associates (1941-)** —— Walter Landor（**德国移民**）与妻子创办，SF HQ, 32 offices, WPP 子公司。**Walter 是最早把 consumer research 应用于 package design 的人之一**——"relying heavily on observing consumers in real-life situations — even soliciting in-store feedback from shoppers regarding label design"。"The package itself must do the talking" = **1940s 民族志 / human-centred research 在 IDEO 之前的一代**。Klamath ferryboat 1960s-80s 总部 = 公司 symbol
- **Salone del Mobile (1961-)** —— 1961 Federelegno-Arredo 创办意大利家具商。现 230,000 m², 2,500 公司, 270,000+ 访客 150+ 国, SaloneSatellite (1998, Marva Griffin 创办) 700 年轻设计师。**Biennial 结构**: Euroluce (odd) + EuroCucina (even)
- **Chip Kidd (b. 1964)** —— Knopf VP + 艺术总监 自 1986，~75 covers/year，5 Eisner，2014 AIGA Medal。*Time Out NY*: "the history of book design can be split into two eras: before graphic designer Chip Kidd and after"。**Michael Crichton *Jurassic Park*** 封面 → 1993 电影。Oliver Sacks + Tony Millionaire 合同条款**要求 Kidd**。原则 = **anti-signature**："A signature look is crippling… the simplest and most effective solutions aren't dictated by style"
- **Maira Kalman (b. 1949, Tel Aviv)** —— 以色列裔美籍插画师/作家/设计师。丈夫 **Tibor Kalman**（"We met in this class of misfits in summer school… 'I've known you for a thousand years'"），Tibor 1979 创办 M&Co.，Maira 不正式在那工作但"offered ideas, suggestions, and support"。M&Co. 客户：*Interview* / Restaurant Florent / Talking Heads / National Audubon Society / MoMA。Tibor 1999 去世后 Maira 产 20+ 书。**New Yorker 自 1995 贡献者**；2001 *New Yorkistan* (with Rick Meyerowitz) cover 空空 newsstand
- **Paola Antonelli (b. 1963, Sassari)** —— MoMA 高级策展人 自 1994；**没做过建筑师**。*TIME* "one of the 25 most incisive design visionaries in the world"。**已把 video games 加进 MoMA 永久馆藏 + 推过 Boeing 747**。展：*Mutant Materials* (1995) / *Workspheres* (2001) / *Safe: Design Takes on Risk* (2005) / *Design and Violence* (with Jamer Hunt, 含 synthetic sweat vial)。"all objects tell stories, and some objects… make history — or change it"
- **Erik Spiekermann** —— 德国字体设计师，**FF Meta, FontShop** 创办人 (1999 第一个 online 字体店)。"Spiekermann is famously skeptical of Helvetica" 公认
- **Philip Johnson (1906-2005)** —— 美国建筑师，International Style 推手。**1940s 纳粹同情 + 1938 MoMA "Modern Architecture: International Exhibition" 策展 + 1949 Glass House**。9255 词，**the most controversial**——Glass House 是 modernist landmark，但 1940s 记录 = **Modernist 英雄的"个人瑕疵"档案**

## 5 条具体洞察
1. **YHSong methods-as-message** = 设计师应谈论 *how* 不 *what*
2. **Churchill non-writing origin** = 写作声音从非写作角色来
3. **Landor HCD 1940s** = human-centred design 早于 IDEO 一代
4. **Kidd anti-signature** = "A signature look is crippling"
5. **motherfuckingwebsite**："**Websites aren't broken by default, you break them**" = 4 moves：over-designing / responsiveness as content property / page-as-proof

## motherfuckingwebsite 反过度设计 manifesto
- 反对 over-engineering (框架、JS、CDN)
- "Responsiveness is a **content** property" 不 CSS 框架
- "A website is **page-as-proof** of its purpose" 不设计 statement
- "**Websites aren't broken by default, you break them**"

## Why it matters
- **YHSong methods-as-message** = 对小/中工作室：把自己框为"方法学"工作室，不"做漂亮东西"工作室
- **Landor 1940s HCD** = 设计师应该是观察者 / 民族志者
- **Chip Kidd anti-signature** = 反网红设计师范式（不要"signature look"）
- **Paola Antonelli 推 Boeing 747 进 MoMA** = 策展人重新定义"什么是设计对象"
- **motherfuckingwebsite** = 对 2026 AI 时代泛滥的 over-designed 网站的反制
